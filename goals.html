  • Learn Frameworks: In the next few months, my goal is to become proficient in a specific front-end or back-end framework. I plan to explore React for front-end development or Node.js for back-end development.
  • Attend Workshops/Conferences: Over the next couple of years, I plan to attend industry conferences, workshops, and meetups to expand my knowledge, network with professionals, and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in software development.
  • Lead Development Teams: In the next few years, I aspire to take on a leadership role, possibly as a technical lead or a development manager. I want to guide and mentor junior developers, make architectural decisions, and contribute to the overall growth of the team.
  • Build Full Project: Over the next year, my goal is to lead the development of a full-stack project from start to finish. This project will involve both front-end and back-end work, giving me a deeper understanding of the complete development cycle.
  • Code Review Mastery: I aim to actively participate in code reviews and learn from the feedback provided by more experienced developers. By the end of this period, I want to confidently address issues raised in reviews and write cleaner, more maintainable code.
  • Improve Algorithm Skills: In the coming months, I want to invest time in enhancing my algorithm and data structure skills. I aim to solve more complex coding challenges and contribute to open-source projects that require these skills.
  • Contribute to Open Source: Over the long term, I aim to become a recognized contributor to open-source projects. I want to collaborate on meaningful projects, share my expertise, and give back to the developer community.
  • Expand Language Skills: I plan to learn a new programming language that is relevant to the tech stack of the company I work for. This could be a language commonly used in the industry.
  • Specialize or Become a Full-Stack Expert: In the coming years, I plan to specialize in a specific area of software development, whether it's front-end technologies, back-end systems, or a particular domain like machine learning or cybersecurity. Alternatively, I may choose to become a well-rounded full-stack expert.
